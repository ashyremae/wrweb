Reports & Data

The Runnymede Trust (2020) highlighted how Black students are more likely to be excluded, face harsher discipline, and experience a curriculum that erases their history. The Ofsted 2021 review also acknowledged that exclusionary practices disproportionately affect Black pupils.

In 2025, the Youth Endowment Fund published findings on the “school-to-prison pipeline,” revealing that Black boys with SEND are overrepresented in pupil referral units (PRUs) and youth justice systems.

Despite decades of evidence, the cycle remains unbroken.
