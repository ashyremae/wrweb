Voices

Sam’s story is a powerful reminder that exclusion isn’t always overt. Today’s systems use behavioural logs, 'concern' notes, and SEND referrals to push Black students out of classrooms. The effect is the same as in Coard’s time: exclusion and failure.

By analysing lived experiences, we can link individual trauma to policy-level discrimination. These are not isolated cases. They represent a continued institutional narrative of racial and neurodiverse exclusion.
