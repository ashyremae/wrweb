Digital Activism

This project responds to educational injustice through accessible digital tools. The website includes audio features for those with reading difficulties. A TikTok account shares digestible content for younger audiences. A podcast features lived experiences and expert interviews.

Representation is key—but tokenism isn’t enough. This work is inspired by The Black Curriculum and Lambeth Council’s Good Practice Report, advocating for meaningful inclusion of Black British histories in schools.

Digital activism creates space for learning, belonging, and resistance.
